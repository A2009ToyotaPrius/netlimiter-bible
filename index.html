How to NOT Get Banned (and Why)

What it is: Bungie rarely bans for lag-induced damage, but they will act if you make it obvious. This section keeps you off the radar.

  • Never stream/record with limiter overlays visible.
  • Don’t chase world-firsts with impossible clears (solo Oryx, duo Rhulk, etc.).
  • When a new lobby-crash or freeze exploit drops, let someone else test it 500 times—you stay quiet.
  • Avoid typing “limit”, “NL”, “3074” in in-game chat or whispers—Bungie can keyword-search logs if they ever care.
  • Expect NL4/NL5 byte-patch bypasses to be detected in The Final Shape; stick to mainstream builds or you’ll be the guinea pig.
Red flag: Multiple reports + video proof = almost always a manual review. Keep your fireteam small and trustworthy.
Ports & What They Actually Control

Quick primer: NetLimiter lets you choke specific Destiny traffic. Each port below handles a different slice of the game. Choke the right one and the server believes your client, but not vice-versa.

  • 3074 (DL or UL 1 B/s)
    – Damage to enemies, door triggers, wipe-mechanic packets.
    – DL = immortal enemies / infinite DPS.
    – UL = stops you receiving health updates (prevents de-sync on some bosses).
    Hold too long → Spinach (DL) or Arugula (UL) error.
  • 30000-30009 “30 k” (DL 1 B/s)
    – Game-world connection. Blocks “Joining Allies” and lets you walk through load-zones solo.
    ~12 s = Anteater/Baboon kick.
  • 27015-27200 “27 k” (DL 1 B/s)
    – Player-to-player link. Revive tokens, rally flags, True-Host drops.
    Not host + long hold = Beaver error.
  • 7500 “API port” (DL 1 B/s)
    – Backend inventory / reward calls. Was used for infinite rally flags and gun-damage swaps.
    PATCHED – 3 s desync still works for flags, but no loot loops.
  • 433 (DL 1 B/s)
    – Matchmaking only. Blocks solo-queue activities (alt to 27 k).
Pro tip: Limit ONE direction only (DL or UL) unless a guide explicitly says both; you’ll last longer before DC.
Limiting Damage – Step-by-Step

Goal: Keep shooting while the boss can’t register the hits, then force the game to accept all the stored damage at once.

  1. Stick a Damage-over-Time source FIRST
    Chaos Reach (tap), Thundercrash (impact + lightning), Witherhoard, Osteo, Anarchy, Jolt, Burn, etc.
  2. Immediately limit port 3074 DL 1 B/s.
    You’ll see immune numbers—this is expected.
  3. Keep the DoT running; add other guns if you like.
    Surrounded perk = free 40 % buff even in limit.
  4. Coordinate buffers every 10 s (NL5) or 15 s (NL4) so no one de-syncs.
  5. Call “UNLIMIT” together.
    Everyone stops the limit, then fires an SMG / trace rifle to “register” the stored damage.
If anyone sees white numbers instead of immune, un-limit right away—those ticks are about to be lost.
Synthoceps + Thundercrash after-shock = highest solo burst (65 % buff) because Cuirass only buffs the initial hit.

  • Vorgeth – Infinite 3074 DL; low HP pool, melt away.

  • Phalanx Echo – Infinite limit, but goblins grant immunity outside of limit; kill them first.

Spire of the Watcher

  • Akelous – Infinite; register damage after un-limit.

Ghosts of the Deep

  • Ecthar & Simmumah – Both infinite; download + register.

Horseman / 7500 Weapon-Swap Damage

What it does: Copy a high-damage gun’s stats onto another weapon (e.g., slug shotgun → 4th Horseman) for one monster shot.

  1. Hold your “main” gun (the one you’ll keep, e.g., 4th Horseman).
  2. Limit port 7500 DL 1 B/s, wait 3 s (you’ll see activity drop to almost zero).
  3. Swap to “multiplier” gun (slug, fusion, etc.).
  4. Register the swap: tap text-chat, Divinity bubble, or inventory back-and-forth.

Best combos: Duality→slug, Scatter Signal→Arbalest, HC→Bow (PvP 2-tap).

Patched – servers no longer accept the damage packet on most bosses; use only for fun in patrol.

Buffering & Desync Explained

Buffering = mini re-connect. You un-limit for ~0.5 s then re-limit. This stops the server from thinking you hard-DC.

  • NL5 → buffer every 10 s.
  • Vision / NL4 → every 15 s.
  • Watch NetLimiter “Activity” pane; buffer when rate hits 0 B/s.

Desync = your instance is too far behind. You’ll stop seeing enemy moves, then get Arugula / Beaver / Weasel. Only fixes: change character or restart the game.

Buffer together on voice call—if even one person keeps limit too long, the whole fireteam can de-sync.
Pre-Damage (Hit Immune Bosses Early)

Use case: Kill a boss before its intended damage window (e.g., Caretaker, Zulmak, Warpriest).

  1. Start any DoT on the immune boss.
  2. Limit 3074 DL until you see “Immune” numbers ticking.
  3. Kill yourself while still in limit (white-bar HP or revive token).
  4. Wait; immune ticks turn into white numbers → damage is stored.
  5. Get revived, un-limit, shoot boss once to register.
If you wait too long after un-limit, white numbers revert to yellow and the stored damage is lost.
Misc. Tricks & Quality-of-Life
  • Rally Banner relocate
    Hold flag prompt → limit 3074 → walk to new spot → un-limit → banner plants there (instant relic super at Templar).
  • Infinite rally flags
    7500 DL 3 s desync → grab flag → repeat 30-45 s. No extra loot, but free heavy/super refill.
  • Dupe revive tokens
    Limit 27k → revive friend → wait 5 s → un-limit. Works for multiple revives in one go.
  • Game-Pause (suspend Destiny 2)
    Freezes memory; skip Joining Allies or stall radiation. Hold F4 (Auto-hot-key script) or use “Process Suspend” in NetLimiter. Bee error if >7 s.
